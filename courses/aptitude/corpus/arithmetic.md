# Arithmetic

## BODMAS / PEMDAS Rule
Operations must be performed in this order: Brackets, Orders (powers/roots), Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction.

Example: 12 + 4 × 3 − 2 = 12 + 12 − 2 = 22. Multiplication happens before addition.

## Divisibility Rules
- Divisible by 2: last digit is even
- Divisible by 3: sum of digits divisible by 3
- Divisible by 5: last digit is 0 or 5
- Divisible by 9: sum of digits divisible by 9

Example: Is 4,752 divisible by 9? 4+7+5+2 = 18, which is divisible by 9. Yes.

## LCM and HCF
LCM (Lowest Common Multiple): smallest number divisible by all given numbers.
HCF (Highest Common Factor): largest number that divides all given numbers.

LCM × HCF = Product of two numbers.

Example: LCM(12, 18) = 36. HCF(12, 18) = 6. Check: 36 × 6 = 216 = 12 × 18. ✓

## Average
Average = Sum of all values / Number of values.

If the average of 5 numbers is 20, their sum is 100. If a 6th number is added and the new average is 22, the 6th number = 6×22 − 100 = 132 − 100 = 32.

## Surds and Indices
x^m × x^n = x^(m+n)
(x^m)^n = x^(mn)
x^0 = 1 for any nonzero x
x^(1/2) = √x

Simplify: (2^3 × 2^4) / 2^5 = 2^7 / 2^5 = 2^2 = 4.
